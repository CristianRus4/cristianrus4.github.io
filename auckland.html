Auckland

Auckland is the city I have felt most part of. Maybe it's because of its kind people, maybe because their interculturality, maybe because it's located in the antipodes where I was born, maybe because it has more trees than concrete, maybe because it's sitting on 48 volcanoes... I don't know, it's the whole of everything that makes me love this place with all my strength. Hard to reflect how great Auckland is, but I'm going to try.

This page contains photographs and other things related to Auckland that I have experienced and seems relevant to me. It's constantly updated with new material and they are usually large files, so I apologize (actually no) if I consume your data.
