Hey, welcome

I'm Cristian. I spent the last ten years designing and writing about technology. I'm obssesed with how it influences society and human behaviour. My goal is to communicate and find solutions from a design perspective to how we interact with technology. If you want to tell me something, feel free to write me an email.

Currently I'm working for Webedia, the leading digital entertainment group in Spain. We generate connections through content that unites, inspires and entertains.

Below you can find a selection of projects that I made in the last years for different companies. Please, note that some content may be slightly modified or hidden due to non-disclosure agreements.
